About Jaxwel Woodworks

My Story

Hello, my name is Jackson Welsh. I create custom epoxy river tables and fine woodworking projects. Each piece is unique and crafted with care, blending natural wood grains with modern epoxy styles.

My mission is to build furniture that brings nature and art together in your home.

In addition to the pieces shown in my gallery, I also take on custom orders. If you’re looking for a table tailored to your space, style, or preferred wood type, I’d be happy to work with you to design something truly one-of-a-kind. Reach out through the contact page to start your custom project.
